Jack Torrance's Daily Musings

Today was a good day. I got up early, had a delicious breakfast of unbuttered toast and half of a grapefruit with some runny eggs.

I did some writing after breakfast, also. Here's a sample:

All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy.

Pretty good, right?

In the afternoon I took a walk in the hedge maze. Voices of souls long dead, screaming in their eternal horror, sounded in the murky corners of my brain, begging for release from their unending torment. I could do nothing for them.

I wonder what Danny's up to.
